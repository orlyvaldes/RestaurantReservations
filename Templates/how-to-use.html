How to use?

Once you log in, the lists of available restaurants will be displayed, where you can choose the one you want to make a reservation.

Steps:

  1. Select the restaurant and press "View Restaurant"
  2. Then, in the selected restaurant, click on "Make Reservation" and choose the date and time you want to reserve.
  3. A message will be displayed indicating whether the reservation was saved correctly or not.

Checks

After you make the reservation, a message is displayed indicating whether the reservation was made correctly or if, on the contrary, it could not be made because there is already a previous reservation at that date and time, so your reservation will not be saved.

Your Reservations

If the reservation was saved successfully then it will be saved in the restaurant's reservations, and will appear in your "Your Reservations" section.
